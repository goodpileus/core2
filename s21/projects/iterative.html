iterative project

make 10 new iterations of your thek responses, using different mediums

notes:

  1. for each iteration, use a different medium than what you originally responded with
  2. you can iterate on 10 different responses or select a few to iterate on multiple times
  3. use at least 3 different/new mediums in your responses

scattered ideas for other mediums:

  • found sound or recording
  • short video
  • photograph
  • mixtape
  • sculpture (from household objects?)
  • postcard or some kind of letter
  • text message
  • comment on a youtube video
  • knitting/sewing/embroidery...
  • animation
  • dance
  • flower arrangement
  • food!
  • writing
  • clothing
  • jewelry/wearable
  • painting
  • meme
  • collage
  • small installation
  • something else!

consider:

  • how much of the original question or response do you want to preserve?
  • use the original Thek question and your response as a jumping off point—don't let it trap you with its specificity... how far can you move away from the original question while maintaining some connection?
  • what do you hide or keep to yourself?
  • what is a way of responding to the question without words?
  • what is a way of responding to the question with words that don’t seem to answer it to an outside observer but do for you?
  • what is something that you would really like to learn how to make/work with? try to work with that!
  • work with a medium that you really don’t like, but see if you can find a way to work with that is enjoyable or exciting...
