a final vision

(or a first glimpse...)

We have meandered quite a ways in this class... Having followed the ghost of process and stumbled upon our own worlds...

Now comes the time to create one final (at least for this class...) gesture in illustrating that world.For this project, come up with some way to collect, combine, organize, present, or communicate your world in a solid, comprehensive way.

Some ideas:

  • a collection of artifacts organized into an archive or grouping
  • a publication
    • fiction/nonfiction?
    • religious text?
    • children’s book?
    • journal?
  • a video/animation
    • instructional?
    • educational?
    • propaganda?
    • entertainment?
  • a website (or mockup of possible site)
    • does the internet/web exist in your world?
    • how might it be different from our internet?
    • what might digital communication look like for your world?
  • a tool/form of technology
    • a physical tool or a mental tool?
    • an app?
    • consider: even things such as making a fire and language are technologies in our world… what might a technology unique to your world look like?
  • something else...

Think about how this vessel might also become an artifact from the world — how does its form, organization, logic relate to that of your world? How does it establish and reinforce the context of your world?

Maybe you take one of your artifacts as a model and expand it into a much larger project.

We will be spending the final 5 weeks of class working on this, so the scope for this project should be larger and more comprehensive than anything we’ve worked on in the past. Of course, that sense of scope will be unique relative to everyone’s individual concept and approach and we will work together to define that individually as we work through it.
