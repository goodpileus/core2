meta world project

create a 130x130px image (jpg, png, gif) for the meta world space.

this can be whatever you want. think of this as an emblem or a doorway to your world… what would that look like? maybe it is a shape, a picture, a word, an animation, transparent, or solid...

the doorways will link to your projects. where each doorway goes will depend on your project. if your project exists as a website it will go there. if it exists offline it can point to wherever you have your project documented—possibly: your blog, a separate web space for documentation of the project, or you could send me your documentation and I could make a quick page to host the images.

we will also want to come up with some name to collectively refer to the space… maybe a name for your cohort? sound off in the discord about this :-)

a working draft of the space is here so you can get an idea of how your doorway might relate to others in the space.
