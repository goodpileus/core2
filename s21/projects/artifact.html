artifact project

create 2 artifacts from your world—
1 personal/private, 1 communal/public

consider:

  • how can you “show” your world without explicitly telling?
  • what kinds of artifacts would your world produce? what is important, what is lasting, what is common? or maybe: what is uncommon, hidden, clandestine?
  • an artifact can be anything...
    • vessel
    • story
    • art
    • trinket
    • flag
    • timepiece
    • poster/flyer
    • sound
    • documents
    • video
    • food
    • technology
    • tool
    • clothing, accessory
    • etc...
