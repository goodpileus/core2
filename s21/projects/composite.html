composite project

due 2/15

notes:

  1. select and compile at least 15 of your responses into a singular form
  2. you can alter, edit, or modify any and all of the responses to work them into a cohesive form
  3. generate some form of context (explicit or not) for the composition

possibile forms:

  • poster (or maybe “large digital file with no constraints”?)
  • video
  • zine
  • comic
  • animation
  • webpage
  • slide presentation/performance?
  • something else?

consider:

  • how are you collecting them? are you weaving a narrative or a story? is it guided by vibe? is there a larger context?
  • what can you add to the mix to bring things together?
  • what other materials can you consider using in the composition? what to include from your process, peripherals, context?
  • what are some ways to harmonize the disparate responses? what are some ways to destabilize them?
